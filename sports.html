Raina in 2021 IPL

Former India cricketer Suresh Raina, who had missed the Indian Premier League 2020, is "super excited" about joining the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) squad ahead of this year's tournament.

Raina had missed the 13th edition of the cash-rich league after pulling out of the tournament due to personal reasons. The star batsman has been a key player for CSK and in his absence, the team had a dismal outing in last year's IPL. The Chennai-based franchise finished seventh in the IPL points table but with Raina set to return to the fold, CSK will be looking to get back to winning ways in IPL 2021.
